The 39 Clues Series (11 books)

There are 11 primary works and 14 total works in the The 39 Clues Series

The 39 Clues books feature 14-year-old Amy Cahill and her younger brother Dan on a quest to discover the secret behind their family's power.

The first 10 books follows the Clue Hunt.
The 11th transitions to the spin-off series.
Two companion novels just add depth to the series.

This Series Currently Has 5 Spin Off Series:
*The 39 Clues: Cahills vs. Vespers
*The 39 Clues: Rapid Fire
*The 39 Clues: The Cahill Files
*The 39 Clues: Unstoppable
