The Edge Chronicles (chronological) Series (12 books)

There are 12 primary works and 17 total works in the The Edge Chronicles (chronological) Series

The Edge Chronicles is an internationally best-selling fantasy series by co-authors Chris Riddell and Paul Stewart, which has featured on the UK and the New York Times best-seller lists and sold more than 3 million copies. There are now 12 titles and four trilogies in the series, but each book is a stand-alone adventure, so you can read The Edge Chronicles in any order you choose. The 13th book, The Descenders, is currently being written.
