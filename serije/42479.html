The Children of the Red King Series (8 books)

There are 8 primary works and 15 total works in the The Children of the Red King Series

Also known as:
* Charlie Bone
* Charli Bon
* Medianoche Para Charlie Bone
* Charlie-Bone-Abenteuer
* Die Kinder des roten Königs

This series tells the story of Charlie Bone and other magical descendants of the Red King and of the magical school, Bloor Academy, which they attend.

The story of the Red King, the ancestor of Charlie Bone, is told in the series Chronicles of the Red King.
